How it works

Simple.
Community-driven.

1

Create a free account

Sign up with your email. Only registered users can submit sites — this keeps quality high.

2

Submit a heritage site

Add any fort, temple, ruin, or historic building. Our team reviews every submission before it goes live.

3

Others discover and rate it

Approved sites appear in the directory. Visitors can rate their experience and leave a written review.

4

Take the conservation pledge

Pledge to protect heritage — by not littering, spreading awareness, or donating to restoration.
